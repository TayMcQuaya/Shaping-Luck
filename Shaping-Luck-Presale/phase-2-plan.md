## The Thesis

Phase 1 proved the model in a warm room: Salim's list + John's LinkedIn, private webinar, 600 public cap. Phase 2 takes that proof and runs it through a megaphone. The playbook stays the same — email tracks, behavioral segmentation, webinar-as-conversion-event — but the field is 10x wider and the social proof is real.

The founding members ARE the marketing.

## Social Rollout Strategy

### The Founding Member Flywheel

Phase 1 members are the secret weapon. They've been in the app, done the matching sessions, have real stories. The strategy:

1. **Week 1 post-Phase 1:** Collect 10–15 founding member stories (video + written)
2. **Week 2:** Marketing firm cuts these into social assets — 15-sec clips, quote cards, longer testimonials
3. **Week 3–4:** Deploy across all channels as primary creative. Real people > polished ads.
4. **Ongoing:** New member stories from Phase 2 early joiners feed the same flywheel

### Platform Strategy

| Platform          | Content Type                                       | Frequency              | Owner                             |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| LinkedIn          | Long-form posts, articles, polls                   | Daily                  | Salim + John (personal accounts)  |
| Instagram / Reels | Short-form video, behind-the-scenes                | 3x/week                | Marketing firm                    |
| YouTube           | Podcast clips, summit teasers, member stories      | 2x/week                | Marketing firm                    |
| TikTok            | Luck experiments, hot takes, founding member clips | 3x/week                | Marketing firm                    |
| Twitter/X         | Threads, quick insights, engagement                | Daily                  | John (primary), Salim (secondary) |
| Podcasts          | Guest appearances                                  | 2–3/week during launch | Salim + John                      |

### The "Luck Experiment" Challenge (Organic Growth Engine)

A free 7-day challenge that runs Apr 28 – May 4, designed to go viral and drive summit registrations:

- **Day 1:** Map your last lucky break (what conditions created it?)
- **Day 2:** Talk to someone you normally wouldn't for 10 minutes
- **Day 3:** Go somewhere you've never been in your city
- **Day 4:** Share something you're working on publicly (vulnerability = serendipity surface area)
- **Day 5:** Ask for help with something specific from your network
- **Day 6:** Introduce two people who don't know each other
- **Day 7:** Write down what happened this week that surprised you

Participants share daily using #ShapingLuck. Best stories get featured at the summit. The challenge is free but ends with: "Want to see what engineered luck looks like at scale? Join us May 16."
