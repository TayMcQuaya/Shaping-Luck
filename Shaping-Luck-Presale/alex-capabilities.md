# Alex — Capabilities & Role

Alex is the secret weapon behind Shaping Luck's ability to deliver on its core promise: **engineering serendipity at scale**. Part growth engine, part AI-powered matchmaker, Alex turns the philosophy of "shaping luck" into tangible, personalized outcomes for every user.

---

## 1. The Magical Web Crawler — Serendipity Engine

Alex runs a continuous, intelligent web crawler designed to **surface unexpected opportunities** tailored to each user's unique profile.

### How It Works

- **Passive Discovery**: Alex scans across news, events, job boards, grant announcements, community posts, niche forums, podcast episodes, and emerging trends — finding signals that match a user's passions, skills, and blind spots.
- **Cross-Domain Matching**: The magic is in the _unexpected_ connections. Alex doesn't just find what you're looking for — it finds what you didn't know you needed. A biotech researcher gets surfaced a design thinking workshop. A burnt-out executive gets matched with a permaculture retreat that aligns with their dormant interests.
- **Serendipity Scoring**: Every surfaced opportunity gets a "serendipity score" based on how far outside the user's current orbit it sits while still connecting to their deeper profile (passions, personality, career trajectory, stated desires, and hidden patterns from onboarding).
- **Timing Awareness**: Alex factors in momentum — if a user just had a breakthrough or is in a rut, the type and boldness of opportunities shifts accordingly.

### What Users See

- A personalized "Luck Feed" of 3-5 curated opportunities per week
- Each item comes with a one-line hook explaining _why this matters for you specifically_
- One-tap actions: Save, Share, Deep Dive, or "Surprise Me More Like This"

## 2. LinkedIn Outreach — Activating John's Network

Alex powers the LinkedIn growth and engagement strategy by helping craft and manage outreach to John Hagel's followers and extended network.

### Capabilities

- **Follower Segmentation**: Analyzes John's LinkedIn audience by role, industry, engagement level, and alignment with the Shaping Luck framework. Identifies high-value segments (e.g., executives in transition, community builders, people posting about career pivots or "what's next").
- **Personalized Message Drafting**: Generates tailored connection messages and DMs that feel human, warm, and specific — referencing a person's recent post, shared interest, or career signal. No spam. Every message has a reason.
- **Warm Intro Sequences**: Creates multi-touch sequences:
  1. Genuine engagement on their content (comment, like)
  2. Connection request with context
  3. Value-first message (share a relevant insight or resource)
  4. Soft invite to the Shaping Luck ecosystem (webinar, book, or quadrant tool)
- **Response Management**: Helps triage and draft replies to inbound interest, keeping conversations moving without dropping threads.
- **Analytics**: Tracks acceptance rates, response rates, and conversion to waitlist/presale by segment — feeding learnings back into the approach.

### The Tone

Every touchpoint should feel like John himself reached out — thoughtful, generous, a little provocative. Alex ensures the messaging stays on-brand: _"This isn't a pitch. It's an invitation to think differently about luck."_

## 3. The 7 Onboarding Questions — The Gateway to Your Luck Profile

The onboarding isn't a form. It's a **micro-experience** that makes users feel seen within 3 minutes.

### Why 7 Questions

- 7 is the sweet spot — enough to build a meaningful profile, short enough to maintain flow
- Each question is designed to capture a _different dimension_ of the person
- The combination of answers creates a unique "Luck DNA" that powers everything Alex does downstream
- These aren't generic personality quiz questions — they're crafted to reveal **what makes serendipity possible for this specific person**

### What the Questions Unlock

| Dimension               | What It Captures                              | How Alex Uses It                                         |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Career & Ambition       | Where they are vs. where they want to be      | Surfaces professional opportunities and pivots           |
| Personality & Energy    | How they engage with the world                | Calibrates the _type_ of opportunities (bold vs. subtle) |
| Personal Values         | What actually matters to them                 | Filters out noise, keeps signal authentic                |
| Quirks & Curiosities    | The random, delightful stuff                  | Powers the "unexpected" in serendipity matching          |
| Likes & Dislikes        | Preferences and anti-preferences              | Avoids dead-end suggestions, sharpens relevance          |
| Fear vs. Passion Axis   | Where they sit on the internal state spectrum | Maps to the Luck Quadrant for personalized coaching      |
| Openness to the Unknown | How far outside comfort zone they'll go       | Controls the "stretch" dial on recommendations           |

### Design Principles

- **No right answers** — every response reveals something useful
- **Deceptively simple** — the questions feel casual but the data is rich
- **AI-enhanced interpretation** — Alex doesn't just read answers literally; it reads between the lines, finding patterns the user might not see themselves
- **Living profile** — answers can evolve over time as users grow; Alex nudges re-calibration at key moments
